About Me

SWE, Ecologist, Policy Analyst

Background

International development professional, paralegal, and ecologist turned software engineer with eight (8) years of customer service experience and three (3) years of international non-profit experience providing direct services to accomplish land management and food security projects by training stakeholders on technical skills to successfully support sustainable development goals. Three years experience in public sector (federal), with a Master's degree in Public Policy.

Interested in the intersection of technology and the public sector. Particularly, with an intent to improve public access to benefits and to provide better messaging to public regarding law and policies. Interested also in how technology can support sustainable agriculture and consequently food security. Adept at distilling reams of complex information for clarity and comprehension, with an eye for improving the public knowledge base.

Please feel free to contact me if you would like to explore these ideas together!
